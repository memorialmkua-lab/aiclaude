# DOOR CLOSER COMPARISON — PRIMARY SCHOOL APPLICATION
**Aluminium Double-Glazed Doors | High-Wind Exposure | DDA Compliant Accessways**
*NCC 2022 · AS1428.1:2009 · DDA 1992*

---

### Mandatory Design Requirements (Victoria)

| Requirement | Standard |
|---|---|
| ≤ **20N** maximum opening force on all accessible doors | DDA 1992 / NCC 2022 Part D4 / AS1428.1:2009 |
| Door closer must include **hold-open or delayed-action** function | NCC 2022 Part D4 |
| **Doorstop** required to restrict end travel and protect hardware | VBA Design Guidance |
| Closer must be **EN1154 compliant** (fire doors: AS1905.1) | AS1905.1:2005 |
